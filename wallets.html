Lite Wallet

Lite wallets that don't require downloading the entire blockchain. Perfect for mobile and quick setup. Lower disk space requirements.

Navio Lite

Important: Lite wallets connect to remote servers and provide less privacy than the GUI wallet. For maximum privacy, use the GUI or CLI wallet.
